About Us

MITS Store is a smart and efficient solution designed to streamline document printing and stationery item recommendations for MITS students. Our platform enables students to upload their documents, track printing status, and receive AI-based insights on peak service hours, reducing wait times and enhancing user convenience.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a seamless experience for students by integrating AI-driven analytics to optimize print store operations, ensuring efficiency and convenience for all users.
